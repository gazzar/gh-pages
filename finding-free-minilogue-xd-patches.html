Finding free minilogue xd patches/programs

Gary Ruben

This is a guide for minilogue xd owners to finding free (as in beer) patches/programs (hereafter called patches), i.e. patches others have made and kindly shared for free.

There are generally two ways to get xd patches:
1. find some patches designed for the xd,
2. convert patches made for the original minilogue (aka the minilogue og).
My covid lockdown project was a free app to convert minilogue og patches to the minilogue xd. Download it here. It is also useful for a few other tasks, such as exploding a patch library into individual patches.

minilogue xd patches/programs

I have my own free patches on presetpatch.com, which is a site for sharing free patches. Note that some of these are full patch banks containing dozens of individual patches. Some patch developers offer free patches as tasters, or out of generosity.

  • presetpatch.com minilogue xd patches
  • Bo Nurmi made a 64 patch library available as "pay-what-you-want," where what-you-want starts from 0 dollars
  • ultimatepatches taster 

minilogue og patches/programs

  • A number of "sound packs" can be downloaded from Korg's website
  • The factory patches for the minilogue og are contained in the minilogue og librarian; this can be installed and the factory patches exported from it, ready for conversion
  • presetpatch.com has minilogue og patches
  • Bo Nurmi made 201 minilogue og patches available (101 here and another 100 here) under the "pay-what-you-want" model

Enjoy, but don't miss out on the joy of creating your own patches and sharing them with others.
